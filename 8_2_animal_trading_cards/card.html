Levi

An active and intelligent dog.

  • Breed: Greek Shepherd / Greek Wolfdog mix
  • Birth Date: December 15, 2011
  • Favorite Activities: Laying in sunny places and sniffing around in the park
  • Dislikes: To take him to the Vet house

Levi is happiest living indoors with the family, but with access to a large, fenced yard, where he can burn off some of his natural energy. He must be kept busy learning, playing, and working. Daily exercise, both physical (such as jogging and Frisbee) and mental (such as training sessions), is a must.
